This chart shows the profit per state for the 50 states in the US. Profits are highest in California. The profit per state ranges from 76,381 in California and the lowest profit for a state is Texas with -25,729. 10 states have negative profits but there is an overall positive number because the positive outweighs the negative.

Here we see the profit for the state of California from May 2011 to November 2014 and the data moves up and down quite a lot. The highest month of profit was November 2013 when they racked in 4,235 dollars of profit. February 2011 was very low and the lowest point during those three years and they hit only 52 dollars in profit. The overall trend look pretty promising because it appears that there is an overall upward trend.

In this table, we see the amount of profit that each product brings in and how much they each contribute to the overall profit of California. Again this is only for the state of California. Technology accessories bring in the most profit for California and bring in about 11,000 dollars. California makes a positive profit on all of the categories except tables.
